# Anthropic Agrees to Rent CoreWeave AI Capacity to Power Claude

**Source:** Bloomberg / HackerNews
**Date:** 2026-04-10
**Original URL:** https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-10/anthropic-agrees-to-rent-coreweave-ai-capacity-to-power-claude

---

Anthropic agreed to tap data center capacity from CoreWeave Inc. as part of efforts to handle increasing demand for its Claude AI services. The multiyear deal helps Anthropic build and deploy its Claude AI models at greater scale, giving Claude access to Nvidia GPU capacity across US data centers for production-scale AI workloads.

The capacity will include a variety of Nvidia chip architectures at data centers in the United States. Financial terms of the agreement were not disclosed. The compute capacity is expected to come online later in 2026, with a phased buildout approach and potential expansion options.

The partnership signals Anthropic's infrastructure diversification strategy, reducing dependence on any single cloud provider. This comes alongside Anthropic's expansion of its partnership with Google and Broadcom for multiple gigawatts of next-generation compute, announced on April 6, 2026.

## Market Impact

CoreWeave's stock surged over 5% in premarket trading following the announcement. The company had already gained over 20% in the previous month amid multiple positive developments. Year-to-date, CoreWeave's stock has gained approximately 29%.

The agreement supports CoreWeave's revenue diversification strategy, as Microsoft previously accounted for roughly 67% of its income.

## Context Within Broader Deals

The Anthropic deal represents one of several major infrastructure contracts for CoreWeave. The announcement arrived just one day after CoreWeave disclosed a $21 billion expanded deal with Meta for computing services. CoreWeave had initially secured a $14.2 billion agreement with Meta in September, which expanded to the additional $21 billion announced on April 9.

CoreWeave also recently announced an $11.9 billion deal with OpenAI and a $6.3 billion order with Nvidia. The company further disclosed that it secured $8.5 billion in financing backed by semiconductor inventory and Meta's AI infrastructure commitments, allowing it to access capital at more favorable rates.

## The Bigger Picture

CoreWeave's growing portfolio of major clients -- now including Anthropic, Meta, OpenAI, and Microsoft -- positions the company as a critical enabler of large-scale AI development. The addition of Anthropic means that nine of the ten leading AI model providers are now CoreWeave customers. These agreements underscore the enormous computational demands required to train and operate advanced language models like Claude at scale.
